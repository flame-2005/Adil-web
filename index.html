Adobe premiere Pro

Adobe Premiere Pro is a professional video editing software developed by Adobe Inc. It is widely recognized as one of the industry standards for video editing and post-production.

Adobe after effects

Adobe After Effects is a renowned motion graphics and visual effects software. It enables users to create stunning animations, visual effects, and composites for film, television, and web content. With a rich array of tools and seamless integration with Adobe's Creative Cloud suite, it's a staple for professionals in the industry.

Davinchi resolve

DaVinci Resolve is a comprehensive video editing and color correction software developed by Blackmagic Design. It offers professional-grade tools for video editing, color correction, audio post-production, and visual effects. With a user-friendly interface and both free and paid versions available, it's a popular choice for filmmakers and video editors.

Filmora Go

FilmoraGo is a user-friendly mobile video editing application developed by Wondershare. It's designed for iOS and Android devices, allowing users to easily create and edit videos on their smartphones or tablets. FilmoraGo provides a range of features such as video trimming, transitions, filters, and the ability to add music and text to create engaging video content for social media and personal projects. It's a convenient and accessible option for those looking to edit videos on the go.

KINEMASTER

KineMaster is a user-friendly mobile video editing app for iOS and Android. It offers multi-layer editing, transitions, effects, audio tools, and text/sticker additions. Available in free and premium versions, it's ideal for creating quality videos on the go.

CAPCUT

CapCut is a mobile video editing app designed for iOS and Android devices. It provides users with a simple yet feature-rich platform for creating and editing videos. With tools for trimming, adding effects, transitions, music, and text, CapCut is a user-friendly option for video editing on smartphones and tablets.
